Protocol Journals Knowledge Base

JOVE
Keywords: JoVE, Journal of Visualized Experiments, video protocol, video methods, visualized
JoVE (Journal of Visualized Experiments) publishes peer-reviewed video protocols. Videos demonstrate techniques that are difficult to learn from text alone.

NATURE PROTOCOLS
Keywords: Nature Protocols, Nat Protoc, step-by-step, detailed methods, Nature Publishing
Nature Protocols publishes detailed, step-by-step experimental procedures. High-quality protocols from leading labs with troubleshooting guidance.

STAR PROTOCOLS
Keywords: STAR Protocols, Cell Press, structured, transparent, accessible, reproducible
STAR Protocols from Cell Press uses structured format emphasizing accessibility and reproducibility. Linked to research papers.

BIO-PROTOCOL
Keywords: Bio-protocol, open access, peer-reviewed, community, reproducibility
Bio-protocol is an open-access platform for peer-reviewed life science protocols. Community-driven with emphasis on reproducibility.

CURRENT PROTOCOLS
Keywords: Current Protocols, Wiley, comprehensive, regularly updated, standard methods
Current Protocols publishes comprehensive, regularly updated protocol collections across life sciences. Standard reference for methods.

COLD SPRING HARBOR PROTOCOLS
Keywords: Cold Spring Harbor, CSH Protocols, CSHP, established methods, molecular biology
Cold Spring Harbor Protocols covers molecular biology, cell biology, and related fields. Classic resource for established methods.

METHODS IN ENZYMOLOGY
Keywords: Methods in Enzymology, Elsevier, series, comprehensive, reference
Methods in Enzymology is a long-running book series with detailed protocols for biochemistry and molecular biology techniques.

PROTOCOLS.IO
Keywords: protocols.io, digital protocol, version control, sharing, open
Protocols.io is a platform for creating, sharing, and discovering research protocols. Includes version control and community features.

PROTOCOL EXCHANGE
Keywords: Protocol Exchange, Nature, preprint, community protocols
Protocol Exchange is Nature's open repository for research protocols. Community-contributed methods with minimal peer review.

METHODS IN MOLECULAR BIOLOGY
Keywords: Methods in Molecular Biology, Springer, Humana, protocol series
Methods in Molecular Biology (Humana/Springer) publishes detailed protocols in thematic volumes covering specialized topics.
